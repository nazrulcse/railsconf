Ruby Central, Inc.

RailsConf and our Fall conference, RubyConf, are organized by Ruby Central, Inc., a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization dedicated to the support and advocacy of the Ruby community. Ruby Central functions as the visible presence and point of contact for these two annual conferences and other exciting Ruby activities.
